1.2 Jonathan Pollard Case: Evidence of Continued Espionage

Background:

  • Name: Jonathan Jay Pollard
  • Position: Civilian intelligence analyst for US Navy
  • Timeline: Spied for Israel from June 1984 until arrest in November 1985
  • Conviction: Pleaded guilty to conspiracy to commit espionage on March 5, 1986
